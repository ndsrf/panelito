# Plan: Management & Hardening

Finalize the Multiverse experience by adding branch management features for the creator and enforcing limits to ensure system stability and performance.

## Requirements
- **BRANCH-05**: Sessions are limited to a maximum of 5 simultaneous active branches (Updated to 50 soft limit per D-13).

## User Decisions
- **D-13**: 50-Branch Soft Limit.
- **D-17**: Creator can "Archive" and "Restore" branches via the `CreatorControls` drawer.
- **D-06**: Manual renaming of branches.
